Biography / 个人简介

I am currently an assistant professor at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), School of Mathematics and Sciences. . My research including longitudinal data, mixed-effects models, hypothesis test, multiple imputation, model-based clustering, and replicability assessment in proteomics-brain association studies.

我目前任职于中国科学技术大学数学科学学院，研究方向为生物统计。 我的研究兴趣包括纵向数据，混合效应模型，假设检验，不完整数据的多重插补，基于模型的聚类方法，以及蛋白质组学与脑表型关联研究的可重复性评估等。
